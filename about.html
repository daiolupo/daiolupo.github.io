About daiolupo

Hi, I'm Dwain Aiolupotea (daiolupo) a Visual artist and Illustrator. I'm currently learning to code and I'm particularly interested in Full-stack web development. I'm currently based out in Porirua, Wellington, New Zealand.

Feel free to get in touch if you need my help on whatever project you may have in mind. Whether its print or web, we can work together to reach your goals. If you would like to discuss your project or just say hello, then don't be afraid to get in touch and drop me a message.
